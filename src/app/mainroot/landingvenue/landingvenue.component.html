About
Suits + Tables is a a marketing platform that:
  • Inspires customers to rearch + book new venue
  • Delivers leads to drive profitability + efficiency for venues offering group dining
How It Works
Customers browse and filter based on specific event requirements. RFPs are then sent directly to selected venues for interest. Venues can accept or reject leads with a few simple clicks
Low Risk
  • No sign up or anunual fees
  • No pre-packaging required allowing partners flexbility in pricing for reach lead
High Reward
  • S + t primarily advertises to corporations to drive Mon-Thurs. bookings
  • By joining S + T, expose your venue to more customers within and beyond your markey with little effort or expense
  • Using a centralized portal allows staff to remain organized throughout the booking process and retarget part customers for repeat business
Simple Sign Up
  • S + T will expedite the sign-up process by building your profile and venues may edit at any time
  • Complete web-base; no extra software to download, no apps, just open your current work email and access your leads
Value Added
